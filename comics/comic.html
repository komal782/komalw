The Basics

Here is how you can create your own comic easily

If you want the doctor to speak, start your sentence with "-"
If you want the patient to speak, start your sentence with "="


Creating a new panel is easy, just add a double space (press return twice) and continue your dialogue


If you want to get your point across without a character dialogue box, type it in normally. (This can trigger keywords)
